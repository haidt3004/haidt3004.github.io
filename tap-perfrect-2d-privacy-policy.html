User Rights and Controls

Users maintain the following rights regarding their data:

1. Data Modification and Deletion

Local application data may be reset or deleted through:

  • Application data clearing via device settings
  • Complete application uninstallation
  • In-application reset functions (where available)

Note: Local data deletion is irreversible and will result in permanent loss of progress, scores, and preferences.

2. Advertising Preferences

Users may opt out of personalized advertising through platform-specific controls:

  • iOS/iPadOS Devices: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > [Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"]
  • Android Devices: Settings > Google > Ads > Delete advertising ID / Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Web Browsers: Browser privacy settings, cookie controls, or third-party privacy extensions

Opting out of personalized advertising does not eliminate advertisements entirely but limits behavioral targeting. The Application will continue to display contextual advertisements to support its free-to-play model.

3. Data Portability

As the Application does not collect personally identifiable information or maintain user accounts, data portability rights are not applicable.
