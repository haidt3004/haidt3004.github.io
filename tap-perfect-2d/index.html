Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I play? – Tap anywhere on the screen to make the bird flap upward. Navigate through gaps to score points!
  • Game crashes or freezes – Try closing other apps and restarting the game. Make sure you have the latest version.
  • Score not saving? – Your high score is saved locally. If issues persist, try reinstalling the app.
  • Ad issues – Ads help keep the game free. If ads aren't loading, check your internet connection.
  • Controls feel off? – The game is designed for precise timing. Practice makes perfect!
